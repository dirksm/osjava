Review

This is the first book available on Jakarta Commons. Every released Commons component at time of print is covered and there are numerous examples. I do have a certain level of self-interest in this as I was a technical reviewer on the book. There are other Jakarta Commons books on the way, and this book is ideal for the newbie to Jakarta Commons.
